What is this?

My Personal Diary is a static, HTML/CSS‑only website designed to look like a cozy notebook—perfect for a design project or portfolio demo.

  • Notebook aesthetic with paper textures, tape, and pins.
  • CSS‑only dark mode toggle.
  • Mood tracker and printable pages.
  • Accessible typography and semantic HTML5.

Tech Stack

Just HTML5 and CSS3. No JavaScript required.

Responsive

Grid/Flex layouts adapt to all screens.

Semantic

Proper headings, landmarks, and labels.

Printable

Dedicated print stylesheet—save as PDF.
